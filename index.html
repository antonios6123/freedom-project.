Causes of Acne

Too much oil being produced in your skin by your follicles.

Dead skin build up in your pores.

Bacteria build up in your pores.

Info

In case you are not sure which type of acne you have, here are the symptoms.

Papules are small, red, raised bumps caused by inflamed or infected hair follicles

Nodules are solid, often painful lumps beneath the surface of your skin

Pustules are small red pimples that have pus at their tips,and finally

Cysts are large lumps found beneath your skin that contain pus and are usually painful.

In case you don't care about your acne, here are the risks of having acne.

Acne is known for causing:

depression

anxiety

low self-esteem

feeling lonely.

Here is how you can treat it!

Clean your skin daily with soap and get that nasty dirt out of there

Shampoo your hair, and make sure it's not in your face.

I know this is a popular one, STOP SQUEEZING YOUR PIMPLES, that allows bacteria into your skin.

Most importantly, stop (I mean it) STOP touching your face!

How can acne be prevented?

Honestly it's very simple!

Wash your face two times every day! (using an oil free wash)

Buy creams to remove excess oils on your face

If you are a female, don't wear makeup that contains oil

Shower after you exercise

Avoid tight fitting clothing, and YOUR DIET!!
